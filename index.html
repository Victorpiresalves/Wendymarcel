Warning Signs of Potential Scams

❌Lack of Clinical Studies

Be cautious of brands that highlight individual ingredients instead of proving the product’s overall effectiveness.
The gold standard for any supplement is a placebo-controlled clinical trial conducted by an independent third party.

❌Unproven Ingredients

Only a small number of nutrients truly support healthy blood sugar levels effectively and safely.

Many so-called blood sugar supplements rely on ingredients with little to no clinical relevance.

Be cautious of formulas that include long ingredient lists just to appear impressive — more isn’t always better.
In fact, some products we reviewed contained over 10 different ingredients, most offering questionable benefits for blood sugar control.
